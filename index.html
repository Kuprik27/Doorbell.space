FAQ

Before you ask

Is $25/day really enough to see results?

Yes, if targeting stays narrow — one city, one service, long-tail keywords instead of broad expensive ones. That's exactly how we set up Starter plans. It won't get you national volume, but it's enough to validate the channel and get your first bookings.

Do you take over my existing ad accounts or start fresh?

Either. If you already have Google or Meta accounts, we audit and take over management. If not, we set up new ones under your own business — you always own the account, not us.

What happens to my account if I cancel?

It stays yours. We never manage from an account we control — everything lives under your Business Manager and Google account from day one.

Can you guarantee results?

No agency honestly can — anyone promising guaranteed leads is selling you a story. What we commit to is transparent setup, tight targeting, and pulling the plug fast on anything that's not converting.
