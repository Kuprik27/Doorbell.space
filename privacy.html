Privacy policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Doorbell Agency ("Doorbell", "we", "us") operates this website. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit or submit the audit request form, and how it's used.

Information we collect

When you submit the audit request form, we collect: your name, business name, service area / city, phone number, and your preferred contact channel (Telegram or WhatsApp). We use website analytics to understand general visitor behaviour (pages viewed, referral source) but do not collect payment information on this site.

How we use it

  • To send you information directly related to services you've requested
  • To improve this website and our ad campaigns

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Advertising and cookies

This site may use Google Ads and Meta Pixel conversion tracking to measure ad performance. These services may set cookies in your browser. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your browser settings or via Google Ads Settings.

Data retention

We retain contact form submissions for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and for standard business record-keeping, and delete on request.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us via @Door_BellSup, WhatsApp, or sup@doorbell.space.
